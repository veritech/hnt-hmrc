FAQ

        
How are these numbers calculated?

        The daily HNT/GBP price is fetched from Coin Gecko, and then the HNT earnings for the provided address pulled from the official Helium API. The number of tokens mined/added in a day is then multiplied by the daily value, and the sum of those daily earnings is shown above.
        
Why don't you have fancy charts & visualizations

        
The helium team has done a great job of that with the helium explorer. This only exists to keep HMRC off your back.

        
Can i scrape your site?

        
I'd rather you didn't, but there is nothing stopping you if you want to. However, If you are sophisticated enough to do that you could probably do all of this yourself without much effort
